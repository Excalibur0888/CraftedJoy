The CraftedJoy Journey

Founded in Bangalore, CraftedJoy began with a simple mission: to create beautiful, educational wooden toys that connect children with India's rich heritage of craftsmanship. Our journey started when our founder, a third-generation woodworker, noticed the declining presence of traditional wooden toys in children's lives.

Today, we work with a team of skilled artisans who combine traditional techniques with modern safety standards to create toys that spark imagination and support learning.

Our Crafting Process

1

Material Selection

2

Our designers create detailed blueprints, combining traditional patterns with educational elements.

3

Handcrafting

Skilled artisans shape and detail each toy using traditional techniques passed down through generations.

4

Quality Control

Every toy undergoes rigorous safety testing to ensure it meets international standards.
